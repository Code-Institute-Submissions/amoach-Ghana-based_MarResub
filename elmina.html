Elmina

Kakum National Park adventure,
Elmina Castle history tour

and Elmina Beach (Full day)

Elmina is a must when you come to Ghana because of all these attractions. Early morning pick up to avoid the Accra traffic and get you ready to explore Kakum National Park -home of the 3rd largest canopy walkway in Africa. It consists of 7 bridges which increase in height as you go along, which level will you reach? You'll be greeted by your tour guide at Elmina Castle who will tell about how it contributed to the big dark history of Ghana. This package also includes all transits.

ITINERARY

Kakum National Park!
The first part of this trip will involve delving into the forest and facing your fears of heights or, just braving the full 330m length and take in the beauty the forest has to offer! Either way, you'll feel like a true African explorer being immersed 130ft high. Once you've gotten through it all, taken all the photos and memorabilias there is to take, we'll head over to Elmina Castle

Elmina Beach!
Just a stones throw away from Elmina Castle, we will end the day relaxing at the beach and catching the sunset. There is also the local bar accessible from the beach at Elmina Beach Resort where you can purchase drinks as well as food.
